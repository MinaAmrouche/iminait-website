About me

I'm Mina, a front-end developer with over 8 years of experience in web development. I have a passion for building projects from the ground up and turning ideas into working web applications. I specialise in JavaScript, using React & Vue, to create smooth and user-friendly experiences.


I focus on quality and detail, collaborating with UI/UX designers and back-end developers to create integrated solutions. With a master's degree in computer programming and certification as a Professional Scrum Master, I bring both technical expertise and a collaborative spirit to every project.

Services

At IMinaIT, we transform ideas into digital realities. Here are some of the key services we offer:

  • Progressive Web Applications (PWA)

    We deliver native-like experiences by turning your web applications into PWAs. Our services include managing cache, providing offline content, and sending push notifications. We can also convert your web app into iOS and Android native applications, making deployment on app stores seamless and straightforward.

  • Front-End Development

    We build dynamic and interactive web applications using modern JavaScript frameworks. Our expertise in React and Vue.js allows us to create high-performance, scalable applications that provide smooth and engaging user experiences.

  • UX/UI Design Collaboration

    We work closely with designers to create intuitive and engaging user interfaces. Our collaboration ensures a seamless integration of design and functionality, resulting in an optimal user experience that aligns with your vision and goals.

  • Custom Web Applications

    We develop tailored web solutions to meet your unique business needs. From concept to deployment, our end-to-end development services ensure that your web applications are built to your specifications, delivering the functionality and performance you require.
