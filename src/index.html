About me

I'm Mina, a front-end developer with over 8 years of experience in web development. I have a passion for building projects from the ground up and turning ideas into working web applications. I specialise in JavaScript, using React & Vue, to create smooth and user-friendly experiences.

My work at Monsieur Biz, Saegus, and UbiOps has given me the chance to understand different client needs and deliver custom solutions that meet their goals. Now, as a self-employed front-end developer, I manage various projects, always focusing on quality and attention to detail.

I have a master's degree in computer programming and I'm a certified Professional Scrum Master. This background helps me bring both technical expertise and a collaborative approach to every project I work on. I love working with teams, including UI/UX designers and back-end developers, to create cohesive and integrated solutions.
